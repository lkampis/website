About

I am currently working as a Game Designer at Creative Assembly on the Total War franchise, where amongst many other things I am responsible for the design of Campaign AI, Characters, Technologies, etc.

I also aim to participate in learning and helping others where possible:

  • Gave a talk on the design process of Total War Saga: Thrones of Britannia at the 2018 Digital Dragons conference
  • Represented the company at the 2018 IEEE CIG conference
  • Helped with a "Makey Makey Competition" for local primary school students

Before my current role I studied Computer Science BSc and Digital Game Theory and Design MA at Brunel University London, and graduated with First degree (Hons) and Distinction respectively.

I have also presented and published my paper titled "Garden of Eden for Artificial Intelligence: How 'The Talos Principle' Demonstrates the Difficulty of Defining Consciousness for AI on the Implied Player" at the 2016 AISB AI and Games conference.
